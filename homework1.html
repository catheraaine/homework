Hello. I'm going to tell you about Sir Patrick Stewart, and why he is one of my favorite people.

First off, Patrick is in a ton of movies and television shows. He is most famous for playing Captain Jean Luc Picard in Star Trek: The Next Generation, as well as the older Professor Charles Xavier ("Professor X") in the current iteration of X-Men. However, he also played King Richard in Robin Hood: Men in Tights, Claudius in Hamlet (twice, actually!), and has voice acted in many video games and cartoons, including The Simpsons, The Elder Scrolls: Skyrim, and The Pagemaster.

Secondly, he is a knight. As in, an actual, honest-to-God, kneeling-in-front-of-the-Queen, Knight. His title is "Sir". Eight hundred years ago he would wield a sword and carry out the King's justice. Now he probably goes to fancy parties just for other knights. How cool is that?

Finally, Patrick is best friends with Gandalf. Okay, not actual Gandalf, but the guy who plays him, Sir Ian McKellan. Yep, two knights that are besties. They are constantly tweeting pictures of their adventures together. Wearing bowler hats. If you click here, you can see for yourself.
